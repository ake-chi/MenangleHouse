Renovation and Interior Design

Menangle House is an interior design company that provides both casual and elegant renovations for residential and commercial spaces in and outside Sydney.

This includes the execution of luxury and functional designs for kitchens, bedrooms, bathrooms and living rooms. Our diverse team also executes projects for commercial spaces such as offices, coworking spaces and high-end retails.
